Our Products

Custom Impex Solutions

We provide tailored export solutions based on buyer requirements, ensuring efficient logistics, premium packaging, and timely global deliveries with strict quality control.

Cereals

Our range of cereals includes Maize (Corn), Finger Millet (Ragi), Pearl Millet (Bajra), Foxtail Millet, Little Millet, Kodo Millet, Barnyard Millet, and Sorghum, offering healthy grain choices.

Pulses

We provide top-quality pulses, including Black Gram (Urad Dal), Green Gram (Moong Dal), Chickpeas (Kabuli Chana, Chana Dal), Horse Gram (Kollu), and Pigeon Pea (Toor Dal), ensuring high nutritional value.

Grocery

We supply high-quality grocery products to supermarkets, ensuring fresh and premium items for retail shelves. Our selection includes essential staples and specialty products to meet consumer needs.

Palm Products

We export premium palm-based products such as Palm Fruit (Ice Apple), Palm Jaggery (Karupatti), Palmrya Sprout, and Palm Leaf, known for their natural sweetness and health benefits.

Rice Products

We supply high-grade rice varieties like IR64 Parboiled, Sona Masoori, Jeera Samba, Ponni Rice, Parboiled IR64, and Steamed Rice (Sella), ensuring superior taste and quality.

Leather Products

Our leather collection includes Finished Leather, Buffalo Leather, Goat and Sheep Leather, and Cow Leather, processed with high-quality tanning techniques for durability and strength.

Fruits

We export fresh fruits like Banana, Mango, Pomegranate, Guava, Papaya, Jackfruit, Pineapple, and Sapota (Chikoo), ensuring rich taste and superior quality.

Vegetable Products

Our vegetable selection includes Onion, Tomato, Brinjal (Eggplant), Drumstick (Moringa), Okra (Ladies' Finger), Green Chilli, Cabbage, Cauliflower, and Tapioca (Cassava), delivered fresh.
